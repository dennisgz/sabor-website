Resources

One of Sabor's fundamental goals is to increase and maintain a good relationship between Latino and Performing groups on campus. In order to further this goal sabor has compiled a list of contact information for organizations in which you, our beloved fan, may be interested.

If you have any questions please contact our webmaster at ddj2107@columbia.edu or Sabor at sabor_board@columbia.edu.
